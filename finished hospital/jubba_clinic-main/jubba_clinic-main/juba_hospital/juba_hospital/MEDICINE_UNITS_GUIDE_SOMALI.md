## **üìä Qaybaha Noocyada**

### **Qaybta 1: TIRO-LA-TIRIN EE QAYBQAYBSAN** (Waxaa lagu iibin karaa xabbo, baakad, sanduuq)
- **Noocyada:** Tablet, Capsule, Suppository, Patch
- **Kaydka:** Ugu Weyn = strips/baakad, Kale = xabbo furan
- **POS:** Waxaa lagu iibin karaa xabbo, strip, ama sanduuq

### **Qaybta 2: MUGGA-KU-SALAYSAN** (Waxaa lagu iibin karaa ml, dhalaad)
- **Noocyada:** Syrup, Drops, Bottle
- **Kaydka:** Ugu Weyn = dhalaad, Kale = ml furan
- **POS:** Waxaa lagu iibin karaa ml ama dhalo

### **Qaybta 3: UNITS BUUXDA KALIYA** (Ma goyn karo)
- **Noocyada:** Injection, Cream, Ointment, Gel, Inhaler, Powder, Sachet, Spray
- **Kaydka:** Ugu Weyn = units, Kale = 0 (ma furan karo)
- **POS:** Kaliya waxaa lagu iibin karaa units buuxda

## **üéØ SIDA LOO ISTICMAALO HAGAHAN**

### **Tallaabada 1: Ogow Nooca Dawadaada**
Fiiri qaabka jirka ee dawada oo hel nooca ku habboon ee kore.

### **Tallaabada 2: Raac Tusaalaha**
Isticmaal si sax ah qaabka lagu muujiyay tusaalayaasha nooca dawada.

### **Tallaabada 3: Ku Dar Nidaamka**
1. Ku dar Dawada Maamulka Dawada
2. Ku dar Kaydka Maamulka Kaydka
3. Tijaabin iibinta POS-ka

### **Tallaabada 4: Xaqiiji**
Hubi in:
- Dawada si sax ah u keydiyo
- Kaydku muujiyo kaydka saxda ah
- POS-ku muujiyo ikhtiyaarada iibinta saxda ah
- Qiimaha si sax ah u xisaabiyo

1. ‚ùå **Ha isku qaldin noocyada**
   - Ha isticmaalin "Tablet" dareeryada
   - Ha isticmaalin "Syrup" dawada adag

2. ‚ùå **Ha ka taggin meelaha madhan**

3. ‚ùå **Ha ku darin alaabta furan noocyada aan goyn karin**
   - Cirbadaha, Subagga, Inhalers ma yeelan karaan Tirada Kale
   - Kaliya isticmaal Tirada Ugu Weyn kuwan

4. ‚ùå **Ha illoobin inaad dejiyo cabbirka_unit**
   - Tani waa muhiim xisaabinta kaydka
   - Waa inay u dhigmaan "xabbo strip-ka" ama "ml dhalada"
